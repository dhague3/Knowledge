📋 Your AI Readiness Checkpoint

🏗️
Infrastructure Maturity
  • Do you have cloud architecture that can scale with AI workloads?
  • Are your data pipelines structured and reliable?
  • Do you have enterprise-grade security protocols in place?
  • Can your systems integrate with external AI services?
📊
Data Foundation
  • Is your data clean, accessible, and well-governed?
  • Do you have a unified data model across departments?
  • Can you easily extract and transform data for AI training?
  • Do you have data quality monitoring in place?
👥
Organizational Change
  • Does leadership actively support AI transformation?
  • Do you have change management processes for new technology?
  • Is your team culturally ready to adopt AI tools?
  • Do you have AI champions within your organization?
🎯
Strategic Alignment
  • Do you have clear, measurable AI objectives?
  • Have you identified specific use cases for AI implementation?
  • Do you have budget allocated for AI initiatives?
  • Can you measure and track AI performance improvements?
